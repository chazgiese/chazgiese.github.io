On-Air App

On-Air is the internal name for the app strategy in development at Alpha Media. It's a singular portal for 200+ radio stations in their portfolio.

My Role

  • Concept Development
  • Product Designer

Problems

  • Station content is siloed and distributed across many sites
  • Only way for users to keep up with their stations in one place is social networks outside the control of Alpha Media
  • Discovery of new content is difficult and time consuming
  • When listening on the station website and something comes on the user doesn't want to listen to they leave

Goals

  • Bring the experience of station flipping to mobile
  • Allow you to stay within Alpha Media's content while discovering new things
  • Keep up with the latest from all your favorite stations in one place
  • Engage with stations in new ways

The Radio Experience

On the first launch of the app local stations are browsable, creating a metaphor similar to the traditional radio experience. If a user is listening to a hip-hop station and wants to skip the song it switches to another one of Alpha Media's hip-hop stations, regardless of location. This creates live and human DJ'd experience, all while keeping users within Alpha Media's content.

The Feed

The feed allows a user to see all content from the stations they follow in realtime.

Station Profiles

Each station has their own space within the app that. This is a singular space to enjoy all the content that a station has to offer.
