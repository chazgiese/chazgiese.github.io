Plus/Minus

Time tracking apps are feature-bloated, ugly, and most of all: not fun to use! Plus/Minus is designed to encourage you to track your time doing various activities. [WORK IN PROGRESS]

My Role
  • Product Designer
  • Software Engineer

Problems

  • Current apps try to do too much, and look bad while doing it
  • Too many steps from launching app to tracking time

Goals

  • Fun to use, delight the user wherever possible
  • Have a singular purpose
  • Be beautiful!

Layout

I wanted to explore a new way of displaying list items. After creating a few prototypes of different grid systems I showed them to friends, the feedback was clear: a side scrolling hexagonal grid was the most fun to use.
